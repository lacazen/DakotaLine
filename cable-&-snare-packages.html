Snare Cable

Dakotaline cable

Dakotaline cable is some of the most reliable cable there is in the market today. The Korean cable is made with strict tolerances that are adhered to, making some of the straightest cable by far. Wither you use the 7x7 or the 1x19 , Dakotaline cable provides for a fray free cable that stands up to the elements. Dakotaline cable is certified straight with no twists or bending giving the trapper a smooth, quick deployment every time, guaranteed.
