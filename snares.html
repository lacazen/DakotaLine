DAKOTALINE 1X19 SNARES

1x19 cable offers some advantages and some disadvantages over 7x7 cable. 1x19 is stiffer and holds a nice round loop. It is also harder for coyotes to chew through and frays a little less easily than 7x7 cable, which allows us to use smaller cable (less visible) for coyotes. However, the stiffness of it is a little easier for the critter to feel. It probably feels less like a twig or small branch than 7x7. It's all about preference. Mark
